Mingyu Zhao

Ph.D. Student · Rutgers University · mz751@scarletmail.rutgers.edu
GitHub · Google Scholar · LinkedIn

I am a Ph.D. Student in Computer Science at Rutgers University, New Brunswick, advised by Professor Dimitris N. Metaxas. My research interests mainly include Computer Vision (e.g., recognition, classification, segmentation, and 3D reconstruction), Multimodal Large Language Models, Efficient AI, and Multi-step Reasoning with LLMs. I am also interested in diffusion language models. I earned my B.S. degree in Artificial Intelligence from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC).
